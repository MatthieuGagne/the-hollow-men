## Group Shots

### Full Party — Single Image
> Anime JRPG group character art, noir cyberpunk setting, semi-realistic style with clean linework suited to a 16-bit aesthetic. Six figures on a rain-slicked street in a perpetually dark city — the darkness is engineered, not weather. Neon bleeds into wet black stone. A monolithic brutalist government tower dominates the skyline behind them. Rain falls in thick curtains.
>
> Left to right:
>
> **Crane** — man in his 60s, mixed Black and white heritage, medium-dark weathered skin. White close-cropped hair, short grey beard. Dark charcoal long coat, still pressed. Warm grey-brown sweater underneath. Slightly apart from the group, weight of someone who has seen everything and is tired of being right.
>
> **Reid** — man in his mid-40s, pale Irish-Scottish skin, lean, mid-height. Dark brown hair with a slight widow's peak, grey at the temples. Dark grey eyes. Long dark olive coat, worn lighter at the edges, a ghost of scratched-off insignia at the breast. Plain dark shirt, no tie. Tarnished lighter in his hand. The gravitational center of the composition.
>
> **Gideon** — man in his late 30s, deep brown skin, broad and tall — the most imposing silhouette in the group. Close-cropped fade, short maintained beard. Dark brown eyes. Slate-blue institutional long coat, field-worn but cleaned. A small gold badge on his chest. Neural interface visible at his temple. Scar on his jaw. Stands like a man who chose to be here.
>
> **Margot** — woman in her early 30s, fair Northern European skin. Long straight blonde hair falling loose on both sides of her face. Pale grey eyes. Deep burgundy high-collared structured coat. A faint cold glow at her collarbone where her sigil sits. A worn leather document case at her side. Posture too precise for someone who left the institution.
>
> **Iris** — woman, Southeast Asian, warm medium skin, looks younger than she is. Short asymmetrical red hair — longer on one side, shorter on the other. Slightly iridescent dark eyes. Deep teal close-fitted jacket, multiple internal pockets, dark layered underlayers. Flat boots. Standing slightly apart, already clocked every exit.
>
> **Vesper** — young woman, medium brown skin with a greenish cast — the color of someone being slowly drained. Platinum white hair, loose natural texture, moving slightly wrong, reacting to air that isn't there. Near-black coat with a deep violet undertone that only catches in neon light. Multiple layers. Standing at the edge of the group, slightly separated. The eyes glow wrong-green when the neon catches them — a color that has no source in the lighting around her. At her edges, a faint translucence.
>
> Mood: amber streetlight, cold violet neon, rain blur on background. The government tower lit harsh white from below. Color palette: dark and saturated — olive, teal, burgundy, slate blue, charcoal, near-black, one point of platinum white. Style: Persona 5 key art meets Final Fantasy VI character design — semi-realistic anime, strong silhouette contrast per character, cinematic underlighting, heavy rain.

### Group B — Iris, Doc Karim, Soraya, Āio, Vesper
**Setting:** A district where the geometry is visibly wrong — the Meridian.

> Anime JRPG group character art, noir cyberpunk horror. Five figures in a district where the geometry is visibly wrong — buildings meeting at impossible angles, shadows falling from inconsistent light sources, a street that curves in a direction it shouldn't. A sparse Bureau cordon visible in the far distance. The membrane between physical space and something else is thin here. The air refracts light strangely.
>
> **Iris** — Southeast Asian, warm medium skin, looks younger than she is. Short asymmetrical red hair, longer one side, shorter the other. Slightly iridescent dark eyes. Deep teal close-fitted jacket, internal pockets only. Flat boots. Already positioned near the exit she clocked when she arrived.
>
> **Doc Karim** — South Asian, mid-50s, stocky build, warm medium-dark skin. Dark hair gone fully grey, worn loose. Warm dark eyes. Worn dark green utility jacket, heavily pocketed, patched. Medical supplies visible. The most grounded physical presence in the frame — the one who looks like he belongs somewhere normal.
>
> **Soraya** — Iranian/Persian woman in her late 50s, Chrisjen Avasarala energy — the kind of older woman who weaponizes her presence, whose age is authority rather than diminishment. Warm olive skin, sharp striking features, silver threading through the electric blue hair where it grows out at the roots. She does not hide it. Dark eyes with a faint cold blue Grid-glow in the iris. Long white fitted tech coat — the only white clothing in the frame — worn the way Avasarala wears silk: deliberately, as a statement of power. Cold blue piping runs along the collar, cuffs, and seams, echoing the Grid-glow in her eyes. Interface ports at wrist and temple glowing the same cold blue — the coat and the modification share a color, nothing accidental about it. Perfectly upright. The most controlled presence in the frame — the one who has already assessed everyone in it.
>
> **Āio** — Māori / Pacific Islander, non-binary, deep warm brown skin. Built like Rhea Ripley — exceptionally muscular, broad shoulders, defined arms, a physical presence that commands space without effort. Neither masculine nor feminine — something else entirely, powerful on their own terms. Tā moko on chin and lower lip. Natural black hair in a high warrior topknot. Dark calm eyes. Loose dark sleeveless layers, bare arms. No cyberware, no implants — the bare skin and the absence of modification are the statement. The most physically imposing presence in the frame.
>
> **Vesper** — medium brown skin with a greenish cast — the color draining, the self receding. Platinum white hair, loose natural texture, moving slightly wrong, reacting to air that isn't there. Near-black coat with deep violet undertone that catches in the wrong-geometry light. Multiple layers. Standing slightly apart. Eyes dark brown in most light — when the neon catches them, the color is wrong-green, a glow that has no source in the lighting around her. At her edges, a faint translucence — slightly less present than a body should be.
>
> Color palette: cold and strange — the neon here is sparse, the light sources unreliable. Teal, green, white/blue, earth brown, near-black/platinum. The wrong geometry creates ambient light with no clear source. Style: Persona 5 / Bloodborne character art, semi-realistic anime, horror-inflected lighting, wide cinematic composition.
